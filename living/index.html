Living in Old West Durham

Important Information for Residents

Durham One Call

Durham One Call provides many services for residents of Durham. If you have questions or concerns that involve the city, this is a good place to start.


Trash Days

The city of Durham collects garbage weekly on Thursdays. Recycling is collected only every other week. For more information on the specific schedules and other information about the city's waste management services, see their information here:

Durham Solid Waste Management

Police Districts

Old West Durham sits in two different police districts (2 and 3), and is covered by beats numbered 213 and 312. See here for more information about the Durham Police Department.


Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) meets once each quarter generally at 7 PM on the last Thursday of January, April, July, and October. The location and other details are given out before ach meeting on the OWDNA List Serve. (You can join the list serve by clicking the button on the right side of the screen.)

COVID-19 UPDATE

Due to the Coronavirus pandemic, there are several changes happening in the neighborhood. Our Spring Board meeting will be held virtually via Zoom and WestFest is postponed until the Fall. Currently, the neighborhood is organizing to help neighbors in need. OWDNA is conducting a SURVEY of needs and we invite you to participate. For those who would like to help out, we invite you to SIGN ON as a "Block Coordinator". Our Block coordinators are asked to be attentive to local needs on their assigned block. Those who already know a number of people on their blocks are ideal candidates. Please follow this link to SIGN ON if you are willing to help coordinate assistance for your local neighbors.

OWDNA Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) Board meets the fourth Thursdays of January, April, July, and October, at 7:00pm. The next meeting is April 23rd, 2020 at 7 PM. This meeting will be held via Zoom meeting software. Details will be announced via the list serve and on this web page. Join us to get more involved in your neighborhood!

Current Board members are: Fred Broadwell, Beth Dray, David Eklund, Charles Kacir, Chelsie Mitchell, Beth Plonk, Cathy Saylor, Vicky Welch, John Wood. You can reach the group at owdna-board[at]googlegroups.com

Upcoming Events

March 29, 2020 - John Schelp will lead the annual History Hike, which is a walking tour of Old West Durham and Watts-Hillandale. This event is a huge hit and seems to grow each year. This is a free event. Meet at 1 PM at Green St. and Ninth St. in front of EK Powe. More information will be out soon on the List-Serve.


April 23, 2020 - OWDNA Board Meeting - The quarterly meeting of the Old West Durham Neighborhood Association Board. Neighbors are invited. The meeting is at 7PM at 923 Alabama Ave.


April 18, 2020 - Spring WestFest! - West Fest is Old West Durham's semi-annual family friendly block party. This event will be at 1025 Lawndale from 4-6 PM. The event will include food trucks, live music, and perhaps other entertainment. (In case of rain, the event will be moved to April 25th - any change will be announced on the List-Serve.)

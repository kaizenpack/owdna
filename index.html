OWDNA Board Meetings

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) Board meets the fourth Thursdays of January, April, July, and October, at 7:00pm. The next meeting is October 17th, 2019 at 923 Alabama Ave at 7 PM. Join us to get more involved in your neighborhood!

Current Board members are: Ginger Blubaugh, Fred Broadwell, Beth Dray, Jesus Gutierrez, Beth Plonk, Cathy Saylor, Vicky Welch. You can reach the group at owdna-board[at]googlegroups.com

Stay Up to Date

Stay up to date on the latest news and opportunities in Old West Durham on any of your favorite channels.Old West Durham has an active email list that ranges in subject matter from local events and neighborhood social activities to politics, the environment and many other topics our neighbors share with each other. Stay in the loop by joining now!

Upcoming Events

October 17, 2019 - OWDNA Board Meeting - The quarterly meeting of the Old West Durham Neighborhood Association Board. The Board will meet at 7 PM on the 20th at 923 Alabama Ave. Neighbors are invited.


October 20, 2019 - Fall WestFest! - This event runs from 4-6 PM at Monkey Bottom on Trent St. WestFest is Old West's family friendly neighborhood party! Live music and food trucks. Bring chairs and beverages of your choice. Fun for people of all ages! An additional feature this year - John Schelp will give a talk on neighborhood history. If you have ever attended one of his history hikes, you know you will not want to miss this!

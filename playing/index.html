Playing in Old West Durham

There is much to do in our area and you should never have trouble finding new ways to have fun! Here are just a few ideas to get you started...

WestFest

WestFest is Old West Durham's twice yearly neighborhood get-to-know-you party! It is a family friendly event held each Spring and Fall and generally includes live music, refreshments, and more recently - food trucks. The date and locations are announced on the List Serve so come on down and bring your neighbors!

DPAC

The Durham Performing Arts Center is one of the premier venues on the east coast for Broadway plays, music events and other live performanaces. More information at: DPAC

The Carolina Theater

Since 1926, the Carolina Theatre has been a showcase for music, film, and live theatre. The building is beautifully preserved and it contributes greatly to the vibrancy of Downton Durham. More information at: Carolina Theatre

The Durham County Library

An indispensable asset, it is modernizing to provide updated meeting spaceand to provide digital media for all of Durham. More information at: Durham County Library

Board Meetings

These are held four times per year, generally at 7 PM on the last Thursday of January, April, July, and October. Locations are announced prior to the meetings on the OWDNA List Serve, so stay tuned for this information.

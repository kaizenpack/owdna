Neighborhood Email List

Join the OWDNA Google Group to stay in the loop about neighborhood issues.

To subscribe to the Old West Durham listserve, send a blank message to: owdna+subscribe@googlegroups.com

Post a message by sending it to: owdna@googlegroups.com

Catch up on what's been said before by browsing the listserve archive: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/owdna

Terms of Participation

The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association email list is a service provided by the Old West Durham Neighborhood Association (OWDNA) to encourage our values of community, harmony, and diversity. Participation in this group is volunteer-moderated by OWDNA board members to serve the interests of the members of our community.

Although moderation will be done leniently to encourage dialog, OWDNA reserves the right to filter and/or block participation it feels is inconsistent with its mission or values, does not relate to Old West Durham and/or our surrounding community, is illegal or inappropriate in any way, or abuses the membership for advertising purposes. Examples of participation that is discouraged include:

  • Advertising for services and/or events offered by organizations and/or people not associated with our community
  • Excessive advertising for services within our community
  • Personal or defamatory attacks on other community members
  • Philosophical discussions not relevent to our community or region

From time to time OWDNA reviews these terms and ammends them as necessary. If you have recommendations for improvements to these terms or the moderation of the group, please attend the next OWDNA Board meeting.
